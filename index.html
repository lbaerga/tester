Marc Anthony, el Salsero

Marc Anthony

Marc Anthony is a famous Salsa singer. He holds the Guinness World Record for best-selling tropical/salsa artist and the most number-one albums on the Billboard Tropical Albums year-end charts.[

Marc Anthony's given name is Marco Antonio Muñiz. He was born in New York City to parents who came from Puerto Rico. His mother Guillermina was a housewife. His father, Felipe Muñiz, was a hospital cafeteria worker and musician.

Marc Anthony got his start by singing backup for other musicians. At first, Marc Anthony wasn't interested in performing Salsa. In fact, his first album wasn't even Salsa, it was Freestyle!
